About Me

I'm Hiroaki Minoura. I study computer vision, machine learning and deep learning. I'm a Ph.D. student in Robotics Science and Technology at Chubu University. Currently, I'm a member of Fujiyoshi and Yamashita Lab where is Machine Perception and Robotics Group (MPRG). My research interests are Trajectory Forecasting, Time Series Analysis, Action Spotting and Image Classification.
